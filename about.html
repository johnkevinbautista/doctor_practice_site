Our Story

Medicine the Way It Should Be

We started Meridian Health because we believed patients deserved more — more time, more attention, and more genuine care from physicians who are fully present.

How We Started

Built on a Frustration, Sustained by Purpose

In 2012, our founding physicians grew tired of a system that left patients waiting weeks for appointments, rushed them through 10-minute visits, and reduced every interaction to a billing code. They set out to build something different.

Meridian Health was founded on a simple premise: when doctors have time to listen, patients get better care. When patients feel known, they stay engaged in their health. And when a practice is organized around patient outcomes rather than patient throughput, everyone benefits.

Today, we've served thousands of patients across New York — and we've never wavered from that original belief. Every decision we make, from how we schedule appointments to how we design our office, flows from that founding commitment.

How We Practice

Our Care Philosophy

Medicine is not a transaction. It's a relationship built on trust, curiosity, and honest communication. That means your doctor reads your chart before you walk in, not while you're speaking. It means spending the time it actually takes — not the time the schedule allows.

We believe strongly in the power of preventive medicine. Rather than waiting for problems to emerge, we work proactively with every patient on health goals, risk factors, and lifestyle medicine. Annual comprehensive physicals are detailed and unhurried. Follow-ups are genuinely followed up on.

When specialist care is needed, we coordinate it thoughtfully and stay in the loop. Your Meridian physician remains your advocate throughout, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks.
