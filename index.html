Why Meridian

A Different Kind of Practice

We designed every part of the care experience to reduce friction, increase access, and put your health first — not the schedule.

Same-Day Appointments

When you're not feeling well, you shouldn't have to wait weeks. We hold daily slots for urgent needs and offer next-day booking as a rule, not an exception.

Longer, Unhurried Visits

Standard appointments run 40–60 minutes. Your time is valuable and your concerns are real — we make sure nothing important gets left out.

Telehealth on Your Terms

Connect with your provider by video or phone when coming in isn't practical. Secure, convenient, and as thorough as an in-person visit for many concerns.

Preventive-First Focus

Great health isn't reactive — it's proactive. We partner with you on wellness screenings, lifestyle goals, and health plans tailored to your life.

Continuity of Care

You'll see the same doctor at every visit. We believe that real health outcomes happen through ongoing relationships, not one-off encounters.

Simple Online Scheduling

Book, reschedule, or cancel in seconds from your phone or computer. No hold music, no phone trees — just healthcare that fits your life.
