What We Offer

Comprehensive Care, Delivered with Intention

From your first visit to ongoing chronic disease management, Meridian Health offers the full spectrum of primary care services — all in one practice, with one team that knows you.

Getting Started

Your First Visit, Step by Step

Becoming a Meridian patient is simple. Here's what to expect from day one.

1
Book Online or by Phone

Choose a time that works for you — evenings and same-day slots are available. Takes less than two minutes.

2
Complete Your Intake

You'll receive a secure intake form to complete before your visit. We read it before you arrive — no repeating yourself.

3
Meet Your Doctor

Your appointment is unhurried. Plan for 45–60 minutes with your physician — time to talk, ask questions, and build a plan together.

4
Ongoing Partnership

After your visit, you'll have direct access to your care team via the patient portal. Follow-ups, referrals, and results — all handled with care.

Specialty & Ongoing Care

Chronic & Specialized Care

Long-term conditions require long-term attention. We provide structured, personalized management plans that evolve as your needs do.

Chronic Disease Management

Living with a chronic condition doesn't have to mean constant disruption. We develop structured management plans for diabetes, hypertension, high cholesterol, heart disease, COPD, asthma, and more — built around your life, not the other way around.

Diabetes (Type 1 & 2) management and A1C monitoring
Hypertension and cardiovascular risk reduction
Thyroid disorders and metabolic health
Respiratory conditions: asthma and COPD management
