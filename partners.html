Our Network

A Network Built on Trust

Great primary care doesn't stop at our front door. We've built lasting relationships with the region's leading hospitals, specialists, labs, and care organizations so your entire health journey is coordinated and seamless.

Why Our Network Matters

Coordinated Care, Start to Finish

When you need a specialist, an imaging scan, or a hospital admission, Meridian stays in the loop — ensuring nothing falls through the cracks and you're never navigating alone.

Warm Referrals

We don't just hand you a name and wish you luck. We make direct contact with specialists, share your records, and brief them before your first visit.

Shared Records

Our EHR integrations mean partner providers can access the information they need — no repeating your history at every new appointment.

Closed-Loop Follow-Up

After your specialist visit, we receive their notes and follow up directly. Your Meridian physician remains your quarterback throughout.

Priority Access

Our established relationships with partner organizations often mean faster appointment slots and more streamlined care for Meridian patients.

Hospital Systems

Where we send our patients when inpatient or emergency care is needed.

NYP
Preferred Hospital Partner
NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital

One of the nation's top-ranked academic medical centers, NewYork-Presbyterian is our primary hospital partner for complex inpatient care, surgical referrals, and emergency admissions. Our physicians maintain admitting privileges and direct communication lines with their specialist teams across all campuses — Weill Cornell, Columbia, and Queens.

Direct admitting privileges for Meridian physicians
Shared EHR integration for seamless record transfer
Priority specialist referral queue for Meridian patients
Mount Sinai
Hospital Partner
Mount Sinai Health System

Mount Sinai's network spans eight hospitals across New York City, providing our patients with convenient access to world-class specialty care close to home. We work especially closely with their cardiology, oncology, and orthopedics divisions — three areas where our patients most frequently need specialist support.

Cardiology, oncology & orthopedics referral network
Multiple Manhattan campuses for patient convenience
